PREFACE

This portfolio is a comprehensive documentation of my journey as a pre-service teacher during my internship at Taloy Sur National High School. It contains personal reflections, my teaching philosophy, detailed accounts of actual classroom experiences, sample lesson plans, instructional materials, feedback from mentors, photo documentation, and narratives highlighting the challenges I faced and the interventions I implemented. Through these pages, readers will gain meaningful insight into the realities of teaching—the growth, struggles, and milestones that shaped me into the educator I aspire to become.

At the heart of this portfolio is the true essence of the Teaching Internship, a journey that bridges what we've learned in theory with what it means to stand in front of a class. This phase of our training goes beyond observation; it places us right in the middle of real classrooms, with real students, real challenges, and real moments of growth. It is during this time that we begin to refine our skills, not just in lesson planning or managing a class, but in learning how to connect, adapt, and reflect deeply on our teaching. While Field Studies 1 and 2 allowed us to observe and analyze the teaching-learning environment, the internship invites us to leap, to teach, to make mistakes, to learn, and to improve. It is where future educators truly begin to find their voice and their place in the profession.

With all of these experiences in mind, this portfolio is more than just a compilation of requirements, it is a guide and source of encouragement for fellow pre-service teachers. By sharing my real-life teaching encounters, strategies, and personal reflections, I hope to provide future interns with valuable insights and reassurance. This portfolio stands as a testament to the fact that although the teaching journey is filled with trials, it is also profoundly rewarding and transformative. It is my sincere hope that the lessons and stories within these pages inspire others to embrace the challenges of teaching with courage, passion, and purpose.
